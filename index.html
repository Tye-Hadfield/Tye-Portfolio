About Me!

Hello there! My name is Tye and if you have stumbled upon this page, I welcome you. I was living in the United States for the last 3 years before moving back to Australia. In that time I was working within a software company called i-Pipeline, which is where I got my first real taste for the software industry. I have been hooked ever since! I am currently attending Coder Academy and will be going into my final term. I have fallen in love with coding and plan to make a career in this industry.

I am a hopeless gamer who enjoys winding down with a nice unheard of indy game or sandbox game and enjoy playing sports. I have played sports most my life and recently finished a season of Rugby League. My wife and I own a small art business that sells gaming/anime pop culture art. This has been a side project of ours which we are very passionate about.

While going through my coding journey I have found a love for front-end development. I will catch myself on CSS tutorials learning new skills and always have such joy watching a web-page come to life. Though I have a passion for front-end, I am a trained full-stack dev who finds joy also in solving the puzzles that back-end can bring forward.
